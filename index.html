About Me

Combining Creativity with Technology

Hi, I'm Shakil Ahmed, a passionate Graphics Designer with advanced AI-powered creative abilities. I turn imagination into visuals that communicate, inspire, and create impact. My design workflow blends human creativity with smart technology to deliver modern, clean, and visually compelling results for brands, businesses, and creators.

Beyond design, I am also an Author who loves writing Horror, Romance, and Thriller stories. My writing style focuses on deep emotion, strong atmosphere, and unexpected twists—because I believe stories should stay with the reader long after the final line.

Currently, I’m studying in Honors 1st Year, Department of English at the National University of Bangladesh. My academic journey helps me strengthen my language, storytelling, and communication skills, which directly elevate my creative work.
